Refund Policy

Understanding our refund and cancellation procedures for airline tickets and hotel bookings.

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

1. Scope and Purpose

Trip Travel and Tours Agency is an online travel agency that facilitates customer bookings for airline tickets, hotel accommodations, and related travel services.

This Refund Policy explains the conditions under which refunds, cancellations, and changes may be requested and processed.

2. Role of Trip Travel and Tours Agency

Trip Travel and Tours Agency acts solely as a booking intermediary between customers and airlines, hotels, and other travel service providers.

  • Trip Travel and Tours Agency is not the issuer of airline tickets
  • Tickets and booking confirmations are issued directly by airlines or their authorized providers
  • Final decisions regarding refunds and cancellations are determined by the airline or accommodation provider

3. Third-Party Booking and Ticket Issuance

Bookings are processed through authorized third-party reservation systems used by airlines and accommodation providers.

Accordingly:

  • Refunds, cancellations, and booking changes are subject to the supplier's fare rules, policies, and approval
  • Processing timelines are determined by the airline or hotel
  • Trip Travel and Tours Agency does not control ticket issuance, refund authorization, or approval timelines

Trip Travel and Tours Agency will assist customers in submitting refund requests but does not guarantee approval, timing, or outcome.

4. Airline Ticket Refunds

4.1 Refundable Tickets

  • Refunds are allowed only if permitted by the airline's fare rules
  • Airline penalties and administrative fees may apply
  • Refundable amounts are subject to airline confirmation

4.2 Non-Refundable Tickets

  • Promotional and discounted fares are non-refundable
  • No refunds are provided for no-shows
  • Some taxes may be refundable only if allowed by the airline

4.3 Airline-Initiated Changes

If a flight is cancelled or significantly changed by the airline, refund or rebooking eligibility will follow airline policy.

5. Hotel Booking Refunds

5.1 Refundable Bookings

Refunds are allowed only if cancellation is made within the stated free cancellation period.

5.2 Non-Refundable Bookings

  • Prepaid, discounted, or promotional hotel bookings are non-refundable
  • No refunds for early check-out or no-shows

6. Service and Payment Fees

  • Service fees and booking charges collected by Trip Travel and Tours Agency are non-refundable, unless required by law
  • Supplier penalties will be deducted from any approved refund

7. Refund Processing Time

Refunds are processed only after supplier approval.

Estimated processing time:

  • Airlines: 30–90 business days
  • Hotels: 15–45 business days

Refunds are credited back to the original payment method.

8. Force Majeure

Refunds due to events beyond reasonable control (including natural disasters, government restrictions, or airline disruptions) are subject to supplier policies.

9. Customer Responsibility

Customers are responsible for reviewing booking details before confirmation. Errors in names, dates, or destinations may result in non-refundable bookings.

10. Policy Changes

Trip Travel and Tours Agency reserves the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Updates take effect once published.

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Prominent notice on our website
  • In-app notifications
